DE4 SIOT Data Dashboard

Introduction: this dashboard displays data collected during my cycling commutes in London, to assess the correlation between particulate matter (PM) concentration and traffic congestion. PM data is collected from a mobile sensing rig, and traffic data is retrieved from the TomTom web API.
